## 1. Purpose
Define standardized operation of the EDMS for controlled document lifecycle activities.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel who create, review, approve, issue, train on, print, or retire controlled documents.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Document Owner:** maintain document accuracy and initiate changes.
- **Reviewers/Approvers:** complete review/approval within assigned timelines.
- **QA:** ensure compliance and oversight.
- **System Users:** follow access/security and signature requirements.

## 4. Procedure
### 4.1 Document Creation
1. Select approved template and enter required metadata.
2. Draft content and perform internal pre-review.
3. Submit to workflow.

### 4.2 Review and Approval
1. Assigned reviewers provide comments or approval.
2. Author addresses comments and resubmits if needed.
3. Approver(s) apply electronic signature with stated meaning.

### 4.3 Issuance and Training
1. On effective date, document status changes to Effective.
2. Training assignments generated for impacted users (if applicable).
3. Completion records retained.

### 4.4 Controlled Printing
1. Only authorized roles may print controlled copies.
2. Printed copy must include control watermark and revision details.
3. Distribution records maintained where required.

### 4.5 Revision and Obsolescence
1. Changes initiated through change control or periodic review.
2. Superseded versions become read-only.
3. Obsolete documents archived per retention schedule.

## 5. Records
- Workflow history
- Signature logs
- Training records
- Print logs
- Periodic review evidence

## 6. Deviations
Any process deviation must be documented, assessed for impact, and approved by QA.
