## 1. Purpose
Define method for periodic review of EDMS validated state, security posture, and procedural compliance.

## 2. Scope
Applies to the production EDMS, supporting procedures, and validation records.

## 3. Review Frequency
- Minimum annually, or sooner following major changes, critical incidents, or regulatory observations.

## 4. Inputs
- Change controls implemented since previous review
- Deviations/CAPA summary
- Audit trail review trends
- Access review outcomes
- Backup/restore test evidence
- Open defects and risk register status

## 5. Procedure
1. Appoint review team (System Owner, QA, IT Admin, Validation).
2. Confirm inventory of current configurations, integrations, and SOPs.
3. Assess whether intended use remains unchanged.
4. Evaluate cumulative change impact and need for revalidation.
5. Verify effectiveness of access controls and authority checks.
6. Verify e-signature and audit trail controls remain effective.
7. Review retention/archival compliance and data readability.
8. Document findings, actions, and due dates.
9. Obtain approvals and track completion of actions.

## 6. Outputs
- Periodic Review Report
- Action plan with owners/dates
- Recommendation: maintain validated state / conditional / revalidation required

## 7. Records Retention
Retain periodic review reports and supporting evidence per quality record retention policy.
